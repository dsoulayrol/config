Glossy World Flag Set (READ ME)
Copyright (c) 2007 Ricky Romero (ricky@studiowidgets.com)

A reasonably thorough set of the flags of the various countries in the
world, translated into tiny 22x14 pixel or smaller representations,
designed for use on websites, user interfaces, or similar environments
displayed on a computer screen.

The Photoshop document is included with this download, for easy
addition or modification to the set.
